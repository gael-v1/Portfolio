About me

General

I was born and raised in Indiana. I have enjoyed learning about and experimenting with technology my whole life. The first time I can remember being curious about technology was when I first saw a touch-screen device. I didn't understand how or why it worked but I just appreciated the fact that it did. Ever since then I explored the world of tech myself, mainly computers and phones and I was self-taught through the help of YouTube. I was particularly

intersted in the specs of these devices. I learned what it all meant and now I give tech suggestions to friends and family based on their needs. Eventually I decided that I needed to learn how to code. So, I started taking

the computer science classes in my school and that is the situation I am currently in.

High School & College
